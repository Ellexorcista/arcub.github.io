21/02/2018

Hi everyone,
This term you will be continuing with group/individual projects. We have a £10 - £15 budget for each group. If there are components outside our budget, we will try our best to source them from sponsors or the University.

Society clothes

Sizes and prices are available at: http://students.aberrobotics.club/shop . We will be ordering and taking cash only up until Wednesday 28th February 2018, which is the deadline for ordering. Phoebe will be managing the orders please email (psh6@aber.ac.uk) or pass orders to her.

Annual General Meeting on the 28th February 2018.

We will be running the Annual General Meeting on the 28th of February, this is when we will be voting for the committee members for the next academic year (2018-2019).
If you would like to become a committee member, the following positions are available:
  1. President
  2. Treasurer
  3. Secretary
  4. Publicity Officer
    5. If you would like to apply for one of these positions, please read the position roles and prepare a short manifesto at http://students.aberrobotics.club/committee
    The deadline for manifestos will be the 6PM 27th February 2018.
    If you have any questions about the roles, please contact Tomos (tof7@aber.ac.uk)
    Positions will be confirmed by anonymous voting on the 28th of February.

    Science week 13th , 14th and 15th of March

    The British Science Week event is taking place in the University Sports Cage on the 13th, 14th and 15th of March. It would be great to have some volunteers to showcase some of the technology and robots we have at the robotics club to approximately 2000-3000 school children from all over Wales. If you would like to volunteer during your free hours, please contact one of the committee.

    Meeting Time and Location Moved - 14th March

    On Wednesday the 14th (during Science Week) the entire club will be down in the Sport Cage between 3:30 and 5:30. Your robots will be on display with the rest of the robotics club, giving you the opportunity to show and explain how the robots work when Science Week opens to the public, we will have some tools with us so you can also work on them if you prefer.

    Guest Speakers

    We are looking for some interesting guest speakers to start coming in during the start of sessions these will include, ExoMars ,Research in Intelligent Robotics, iCub, Idris and a few 3rd Year Projects.
    A draft list of speakers will be added to the website soon.

    Many Thanks,
    Robotics Club Committee
