About Us

The Aberystwyth Robotic Club, supported by the Infinity Exhibition is an after school club hosted by Aberystwyth University.

Usual club times 1:30PM to 3:00PM every Wednesday in Part1 lab (lab 1.00), Physical sciences building.

If you are interested in joining, email Tomos at tomos@aberrobotics.club
